About OGBA

The OG Basketball Association started in Minneapolis with a group of guys who loved playing hoops, but wanted to avoid the drama. We are all about having fun, getting exercise, and keeping our competitive spirit alive.

This is not ISO ball, we still play defense, and we love a good pass when someone is cutting to the hoop.

Rules

  • Bring both a dark and light shirt or reversable jersey
  • Shoot free throws to form teams
  • 2s and 3s to 21
  • Winning teams stays and plays the next team, but if they win two in a row, they are off after the 2nd and the losing team stays
  • Call your own fouls
  • Don't argue about ticky tacky stuff, it wastes playing time
